Candid

Meaning: Being honest and straightforward, even if it’s blunt.

Synonyms Antonyms
Honest Deceptive
Blunt Evasive
Forthright Dishonest
Sincere Secretive

Examples:

  • During the interview, she gave a candid answer about her weaknesses.
  • His candid feedback hurt my feelings, but it helped me improve.
  • The CEO held a candid meeting, openly discussing the company’s financial struggles.
